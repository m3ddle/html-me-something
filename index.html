Duque de Caxias, Brazil

Background

Duque de Caxias is the second most populous suburb in the Rio de Janeiro metropolitan area. Yes that means that in Brazil Rio de Janeiro is a state as well as a city. Just like New York is here in the states.

The Duke of Caxias

The city is named after the "Iron Duke" Luis Alves de Lima e Silva, Duke of Caxias. The "Iron Duke" fought in Brazil's early was for Independence against Portugal and many other conflicts and rebellions in early Brazil. He was fiercely loyal to Emporer Pedro II, who is revered by Brazilians in his own right and several historians have regarded Caxias as the greatest of Brazil's military officers.
